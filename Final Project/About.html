About Us

Prime Steakhouse, a stylish Chicago-style steakhouse offering old-world charm at JW Marriott Hotel Medan is fine dining at its best. This is the perfect venue for an intimate romantic dinner, exclusive meeting or casual gathering among friends, as well as for larger celebrations. Only premium meat cuts are served here from the United States, Australia and Japan. Fresh fish, seafood, pastas, soups and salads, all prepared to exacting standards, are also served excellent service and a fine selection of beverages, wines and spirits make this a fine experience.
